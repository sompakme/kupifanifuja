Given the high net worths that Dixie and Charli D'Amelio have, it makes sense that Charli would begin a relationship with someone who is now part of Kourtney Kardashian's family. Charli and Kourtney's husband Travis Barker's son Landon Asher Barker have been in a relationship since 2022 and they both seem pretty giddy about it. Charli D'Amelio and Landon Barker's romance seems to be going well. Since Landon and Travis Barker have a strong bond, fans wonder if Travis approves of Charli. What does Travis really think about his son's girlfriend and do the two have a good relationship?

What Is Landon Asher Barker And Charli D'Amelio's Relationship Like?

Anyone who watched the second season of The D'Amelio Show on Hulu saw Charli D'Amelio talk about her romance with Landon Asher Barker. According to People, they were photographed hanging out in early June 2022.

Over the next month, they were around each other so often that fans had a feeling that something was going on.

By late June 2022, a source explained in an interview with People that they were definitely a couple. While Charli lives her life online, has a staggering amount of Instagram followers, and has also starred on a reality TV with her family, she is a private person at times.

She sometimes shares that she wants to keep relationships to herself because people have had so much to say in the past. This time around, though, she seems really excited, and she and Landon are adorable.

As of September 2023, the couple seems closer than ever. While Charli D'Amelio has dealt with relationship drama in the past, especially with her ex-boyfriend Chase Hudson, Landon seems to be a breath of fresh air.

In February 2023, Landon and Charli talked about their relationship with Entertainment Tonight. Charli shared how glad she was to have him by her side. From her comments, this seems to be a great relationship that has brought a lot of positivity to both of their lives.

Charli said, "There's been so many things that have happened since we've started dating. Whether it was Dancing With the Stars or his own music journey. And it's been really nice to kind of watch each other and be there to support each other through all of these crazy times and, you know, get to do things like this together and dress up. It's always been very fun."

Does Travis Barker Like Landon Asher Barker's Girlfriend Charli D'Amelio?

Whenever someone starts dating someone new, it's only natural to want their partner's parents' approval. While it's possible that Charli D'Amelio was anxious to meet Travis Barker, she explained that Travis has been really good to her. If she was worried about meeting him or thought that he might not approve, she has never said so. Instead, Charli has shared that the whole experience has been wonderful.

In an interview with People, Charli D'Amelio talked about Travis Barker. She said that Kim Kardashian and Barker treated her really well. She explained, "They have been nothing but kind to me. I'm very, very thankful that he has such a great family around him."

She also said that the couple "really let me in with open arms."

Charli and Travis seem to have a great relationship. In March 2023, Charli explained in an interview with E! News that she feels really good about Landon's family. She said, "His family is incredible and they're so supportive of what I do. You know, I'm very close with my family as well, so it means a lot that we all spend a lot of time together. We always have so much fun."

Both Travis and Kourtney were there for Charli when she was cast in Dancing With The Stars season 31. It must have been wonderful for her to know that they were rooting for her.

In an interview with Page Six, Charli said, “Landon knows that I’m doing this. He’s with me every step of the way throughout this journey. Travis and Kourtney, I told them about it, and they were super excited as well. I think, overall, it’s just going to be really fun and I have a very great group around me.”

Charli D'Amelio shared with Entertainment Tonight that it was sometimes tough to appear on Dancing With The Stars knowing that Travis Barker and Kourtney Kardashian were there looking on. She said, “I try not to look at them while I’m preforming, because it makes me nervous. But it always feels good to see them after, and just get to talk to them, and have them see me do something I’m having a lot of fun doing. They never get to see me dance! So, it’s a lot of fun.”

Considering the fact that Travis and Landon Barker are close, it makes sense that Travis would be so sweet toward Charli. He seems glad that his son has found a positive relationship.

In 2022, Landon explained to E! News that he loves watching Travis thrive with Blink-182. He said, "I guess the best way to say it is he's making s--t happen and doing things that he genuinely enjoys. If that's putting Blink back together, if that's being in the studio, whatever it is, I feel like he's prioritizing what he wants and people are impressed by it and people enjoy it."

Landon also said, "He's always in my corner. He's always there for me as I am for him."

While Charli D'Amelio has talked about how glad she is that Travis Barker has been so nice to her, Travis Barker doesn't seem to have commented on their relationship. He might want to give his son Landon Barker some privacy.
