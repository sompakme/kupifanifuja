What is the name of Gurdas Maan son?

 Posted on July 29, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  198 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Gurickk Maan is the name of the son of Indian playback singer, songwriter and actor mainly associated with Punjabi and Hindi language music and films Gurdas Maan and Manjeet Maan, a producer and director of Punjabi films.

Gurikk Maan’s exact date of birth is not known.

However, Gurikk celebrates his birthday on 21 December.

Gurikk Maan was educated at the Eton College, Windsor, England, UK.

Gurikk Maan’s dad Gurdas gained national attention in 1980 with the song “Dil Da Mamla Hai”.

Since then, Gurikk’s dad has gone on to record over 34 albums and has written over 305 songs.

In 2015, Gurikk’s dad performed on the song “Ki Banu Dunia Da” with Diljit Dosanjh in MTV Coke studio India that was aired in season 4 episode 5 (16 August 2015) on MTV India.

Gurikk’s dad is is also the only Punjabi singer to win the national award for Best Male Playback Singer at 54th National Film Awards for building the entire narrative through his singing of Heer in Waris Shah: Ishq Daa Waaris.

Gurikk is married to Simran Kaur Mundi an Indian actress who made her acting debut in the Hindi film Jo Hum Chahein in 2011.
