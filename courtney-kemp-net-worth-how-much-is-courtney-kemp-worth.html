Courtney Kemp’s Personal Life and Background

In this section, we will take a closer look at Courtney Kemp’s personal life and background, shedding light on her upbringing, education, and relationships.

Courtney Kemp was born in Norwalk, Connecticut in May 1977. Growing up, she came from a trailblazing family, where her father earned the distinction of being the first African American graduate of Dartmouth College Tuck School of Business.

Kemp’s educational journey began with a degree in English from Brown University, followed by a Master’s in English Literature from Columbia University. Her passion for literature and storytelling would later become the foundation of her successful career in television.

In her personal life, Courtney Kemp was previously married to Brian Mawuli Agboh, with whom she shares a child named Arden. However, the couple divorced in 2016, navigating the challenges of balancing personal and professional commitments.
