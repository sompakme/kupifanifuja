Kristin Cavallari Launches New Skincare Line

 Posted on September 23, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  464 words  |  Martina Birk

Actress, reality star, and author Kristin Cavallari is bringing the golden glow of the Uncommon James jewelry collection to an all new simplistic skincare line.  A spinoff of the popular lifestyle brand, the skincare line appropriately named Uncommon Beauty, has debuted a five-piece collection for consumers to revel in.

Inspiration for a beauty regimen line has been nearly a career-long endeavor.  Cavallari explains to Refinery29 how she had always been asked about what her daily skincare routine consisted of.  Especially with breakout prone skin, the Laguna Beach star resolutely approached caring for her looks and skin in order to stay camera ready throughout her growing acting journey.

Cavallari describes the product line as a clean and minimalist collection designed with a mission to simplify the daily skincare routine.  Consumers may opt to try each of the five products to complete the regimen for optimal results or choose which pieces of the puzzle are most effective for their skincare goals.  The whole line retails for $215 and boasts globally sourced ingredients to revitalize the skin.

For starters, the Daily Foaming Powder Cleanser, retailing at $38, is a unique scrub made with papaya extract and rice brand that begins as a powder to be mixed with as much or as little water as preferred to create a more exfoliating or moisturizing sensation for daily cleanse.  Cavallari's favorite is packed with pineapple, Australian plum antioxidant and vitamin C, the next step of the routine is the Pineapple Peptide Nectar retailing for $62 and recommended for environmental skin protection and brightening the complexion.

Another cream Cavallari's line is offering has a multitude of uses and is coconut-water based. The Daily Water Cream, going for $48, is best for day or night and can be used regularly on the face, hands, chest and neck for ideal emollient. For those with a special need for under-eye attention, the line also includes the Triple Eye Effect Cream designed to reduce wrinkles, lines, under-eye puffiness and dark circles, all for $52.  Finally, for only $15, the line's lip balm places the cherry on top with its mango-flavored base and packed with aloe vera, coconut and sunflower seed oil for ideal coverage in time for summer.

While the mom of three admits to her mornings being hectic, the regimen actually allows for her to simply splash some fresh water on her face to get started and come back later in the evenings for a clean wash.  She is proud that the Uncommon Beauty products exclude silicones and fillers that shockingly contribute to the aging process and looks to share the piece of mind with consumers.
