Hyunjin is a South Korean Pop Band singer from Loona band which have in total 12 members named ViVi , Yves, JinSoul, HaSeul – leader, Kim Lip, Chuu, HeeJin, HyunJin, Go Won, Choerry, Olivia Hye and YeoJin. The band is founded by ‘Blockberry Creative’ (which is a subsidiary of Ilkwang’s Polaris Entertainment). The Loona band has also sub-unit names Loona 1/3, Loona, Odd Eye Circle and Loona yyxy. In August 2016, Loona squad commenced their pre-debut project with each member releasing a promotional single over the next eighteen months. Loona released “Favorite” as the lead single from [+ +] (read as plus plus), on August 7, 2018. Then, on Aug 20, 2018, the second single “Hi High” released. Further more, on Feb 19, 2019, [+ +] repackaged as [X X] (read as multiple multiple) released with six additional tracks, including a new single, “Butterfly”.
