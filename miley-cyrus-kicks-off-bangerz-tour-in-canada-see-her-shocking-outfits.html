Miley Cyrus Kicks Off Bangerz Tour in Canada: See Her Shocking Outfits

 Posted on August 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  254 words  |  Martina Birk

Starting off Bangerz with a bang! Miley Cyrus opened her highly-anticipated Bangerz Tour in Vancouver, Canada on Feb. 14 and the shock-happy star didn’t disappoint. 

Embracing her Valentine’s Day opener, Cyrus, 21, started the show by sliding down a giant version of her own tongue in a red bedazzled two piece, with high thigh slits, a white Chanel fanny pack, and a white and red feather boa. 

The former Disney star showed off her now-infamous backside, twerking throughout the night along with her signature dancers, whom she groped and slapped numerous times. 

At one point she climbed atop a gold car while wearing white cowboy boots and a pot leaf-covered one piece that ended in a tiny thong, grinding on the vehicle and spreading her legs open to the audience. She even appeared to be pleasuring herself in one image as the Parental Advisory Explicit Content logo flashed on the giant screen behind her. 

Cyrus was also spotted riding on a giant hot dog, grinning with her crooked fake teeth, and reuniting with a foam finger similar to the one she rubbed all over Robin Thicke at the 2013 VMAs in August 2013. 

As sexual and confident as Cyrus’ performance seemed to be, the “Wrecking Ball” crooner admitted to having some pre-show jitters on Twitter.
