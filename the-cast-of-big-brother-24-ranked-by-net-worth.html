Big Brother season 24 premiered on 6th July 2022 on CBS in the US. This season showcased a mix of contestants from various walks of life.

Cast members get $13,000 extra for becoming a jury member on the reality show. In season 24, many housemates turned into a jury while trying to evict others from the game. Some of the players came from influential backgrounds and well-established careers. It was an added boost on their resume to be part of the reality show, and their career graphs have taken an upward swing since the end of the game show. Many found love and are working together to build their fan following.

Taylor Hale, the winner of season 24, remains the favorite of America. Fans want her to be a part of The Challenge USA: season 2.

Terrance Higgings - $100,000 - $300,000

Chicago's city bus operator supported his dreams of becoming a professional DJ while entering Big Brother season 24. He took a $1000 paycheck for thirteen weeks as part of the jury pay during the show. According to Screenrant, Terrance was about to file a defamation case against fellow housemate Indy Santos for spreading nasty rumors about him after being evicted from the house. This created a lot of furrows among the two contestants and fans of the game. Since the end of season 24, he has leveraged his time in the reality show and turned filmmaker/writer along with his DJ aspirations. His current net worth is around $100,000 - $300,000.

Joseph Abdin - $332,000

The handsome attorney from Florida is currently dating the winner of Big Brother season 24, Taylor Hale. A team player, he quickly won the hearts of the other housemates during the game. As part of the jury, he received a thirteen-week paycheck of $1000. He currently practices law and travels with his girlfriend, and stays active on social media by posting fitness reels. His current net worth is $332,000.

Alyssa Snider - $100,000 - $500,000

The fashion enthusiast and travel vlogger were one of the housemates in the reality game. Alyssa was also a jury and received $1000 as a paycheck per week for the role. She worked out a solid campaign to stay in the game from day one but only rubbed off on the wrong side of the other contestants. She was evicted from the house during the veto vote round. She works as a customer service executive/marketing agent in Florida, and her social media followers are frequently treated to exotic travel vlogs/reels. She recently visited Paris and Rome. Her estimated net worth is approximately $100,000 - $500,000.

Kyle Capener - $500,000

According to Deadline, the unemployed contestant from Utah created plenty of controversies in the Big Brother house during his stay. He was accused of racist comments and forming a white alliance against other housemates. He was voted out for his actions and disgusting comments. After a brief stint in the game show, he is touring the world with fellow housemate Alyssa Snider as a travel vlogger, and the two are dating officially. His current net worth is approximately $500,000.

Monte Taylor - $500,000

The runner-up of Big Brother season 24 took home a $75,000 paycheck, and the fitness coach enjoys being a personal trainer and doling out healthy meal tips on his social media. He also partners with brands for promotional activities and is the founder of Taylor’d Fit - a fitness-based program that allows his clients to get the best of exercise and meal plans to stay in good shape. His net worth has zoomed to $500,000 after the reality show stint.

Brittany Hoopes - $500,000 - $1 million

Brittany was part of the reality series as a certified hypnotherapist and transformational coach. She was part of the jury on the show and earned $1000 per week. According to Cinema Blend, she entered the house as a toy tester for children and worked her way into the game by holding guided meditation and hypnosis sessions. The housemates initially liked her methods and chose to eliminate the stress of the mind games through these meditative sessions. She remained in the game till being one of the contestants, entered the Final Four but soon got eliminated due to the lack of veto votes. She is now expanding her hypnotherapy business through social media. Her current net worth is around $500,000 - $1 million.

Taylor Hale - $1 million

The pleasant beauty queen is the winner of Big Brother's season 24 reality show. She also won America's favorite player title for the first time in the history of the reality game. Taylor took home a $750,000 grand prize and $50,000 as America’s favorite player prize. According to People, she is also dating fellow housemate Joseph Abdin. She is also known as a former Miss Michigan USA and works as a personal stylist. Her combined wealth from being a social influencer and fashion stylist is estimated at around $1 million.

Michael Burner - $1 million - $3 million

Michael represented the LGBTQ+ community on the reality show Big Brother and soon became one of the fan-favorite contestants in the game. He worked professionally as an attorney in Minnesota and was very open about his relationship with partner Hayden during the show's airing. Being part of the jury, he earned a $1000 paycheck as part of the thirteen-week pay-day clause from CBS. He won the veto power numerous times during the game and saved himself from getting eliminated until almost the end. His current net worth is estimated to be $1 million -$3 million.

The season 24 contestants of Big Brother have seen their fair share of controversies, but they have found love and leveraged the screen time opportunity to shape their career paths.
