Julia Roberts' performance in 'Pretty Woman' was so memorable that she is often known by the movie. Born in 1967 in Smyrna, Georgia, Julia Fiona Roberts has earned her place as one of the highly acclaimed Hollywood superstars. She has had a long and successful career in the film industry and has become one of the favorite classic performers of all time. She also has a staggering net worth of $250 million.

Life Before Hollywood

Julia practically had no prior acting training, although her parents were part of a theatrical workshop for the armed forces. Her parents, Betty Lou Bredemus and Walter Grady Roberts, co-founded this workshop called the Atlanta Actors and Writers Workshop.

Roberts did have relatives who were actors like his brother, Eric Roberts, and niece Emma Roberts. But she did not have any contact with them until 2004. After her parents' divorce, Roberts moved to Georgia and finished her education at Campbell High School.

Julia did get started on her University education at Georgia State University, but she soon realized that she had a knack for acting. She left University to move to New York to be an actress.

The Making of a Star

The star's debut performance was in the 1988 film 'Satisfaction.' The movie was starring Liam Neeson and Justine Bateman, where Julia only played a side role. She also appeared in a 1987 TV show called 'Crime Story' and then in a 1988 independent film called 'Mystic Pizza.'

Julia Roberts made her mark in the movie industry with her performance in 'Steel Magnolias.' Although she was only a last-minute replacement for Meg Ryan in the movie and played a supporting role, it earned her considerable recognition and an Academy Award nomination in Best Supporting Actress. She also received her first Golden Globe win for Motion Picture Best Supporting Actress, as stated by Celebrity Net Worth.

Julia Roberts was once the lowest paid and least known artist in showbiz as her contemporaries were legends like Shirley MacLaine, Sally Field, Olympia Dukakis, Dolly Parton, and Daryl Hannah. But soon, she left them all behind to become the most loved actress in Hollywood. By the age of 23 years, the star was earning millions for every role she played. For example, she made about $7 million for her part in 'Renegades,' as stated by Los Angeles Times.

She was known for starring alongside Richard Gere in 'Pretty Woman,' which became Disney's highest-grossing movie ever and earned her her second Golden Globe Award, turning her into a superstar overnight. The film 'Flatliners' also made her a sumptuous $500,000 where she starred alongside Kiefer Sutherland.

Julia Roberts's Best Movies of the 1990s

The 1990s turned to be a lucrative phase for the actress. She made three movies in 1991. The first one was titled 'Sleeping With the Enemy,' and it was an insane hit at the box office. According to Insider, Roberts earned $3 million from the second movie, 'Dying Young.' The third one was a sensation. She acted alongside Robin Williams and Dustin Hoffman. It was a masterpiece called 'Hook' and was directed by Steven Spielberg.

In 1993 the talented actress starred alongside Denzel Washington in The Pelican Brief. The years 1994-1996 were even more prosperous as Julia acted in six films including 'I Love Trouble,' and 'Something to Talk About.' Her guest appearance in the famous American sitcom 'Friends' made the show more popular amongst fans.

Julia won her third Global Globe award in 1997 for her phenomenal performance in the famous movie 'My Best Friend's Wedding,' where she starred alongside Dermot Mulroney and Cameron Diaz. She had also acted in another film called 'Conspiracy Theory' with Mel Gibson.

From 1990-2000, Julia Roberts reached the pinnacle of success with consecutive blockbusters. one of them was the favorite rom-com called 'Notting Hill' where she co-starred with Hugh Grant; this movie grossed over $365 million worldwide. 'The Runaway Bride' was also a significant success for Roberts as fans already loved her screen presence with Richard Gere ever since 'Pretty Woman.' The movie earned about $300 million worldwide.

The actress earned her first Academy Award with her 2000 performance in 'Erin Brockovich.' She received another Golden Globe in the category of Best Actress for playing the role of Erin Brockovich in the movie.

Roberts has purchased several luxury estates worldwide with her impressive incomes; she has two mansions in Malibu. One in Los Angeles, one in New York, and a ranch in New Mexico. Her endorsement deals with Gianfranco Ferrè, and Lancôme has generously added to her jaw-dropping net worth of $150 million.
