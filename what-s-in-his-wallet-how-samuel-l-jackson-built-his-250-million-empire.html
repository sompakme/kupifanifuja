The 71-year-old Pulp Fiction star Samuel Leroy Jackson has had an extensive career in Hollywood. He was born in Washington, DC in 1948. He began acting in the 70s, and although he would rise to fame in 1994 through his superhit movie, Pulp Fiction, his talent was seen from the start. Following that breakout performance, the star made one blockbuster movie after another and gained attention while also building a magnificent empire of $250 million. His most notable films have an average gross of approximately $89.9 million. His career as a TV producer has also contributed to this extravagant wealth.

Here is how Jackson built his empire of millions.

Modest Beginnings

Samuel grew up under the strict supervision of his grandmother. He got adequate exposure to the world of cinema while learning about the importance of Black presence on the big screen, which inspired him to be a film star.

Jackson's childhood and adolescence were nurtured with the ideals of Black representation in mainstream media, and he was also involved in Black activism from a young age. According to Biography, he developed a particular knack for acting after sitting through a memorable performance of the Negro Ensemble Company and soon began to pursue an acting course. He received a degree in 1972.

A Dazzling Acting Career

Jackson got himself an early-stage career when he was part of the theater alongside his future wife, LaTanya Richardson. He decided to get out of the political theater and upgrade Hollywood. As a result, the actor moved to New York in 1976 to pursue a real career in acting. He started with Off-Broadway Productions and got a chance to act in Richard Wesley's 'The Mighty Gents.'

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Jackson was introduced to the famous director Spike Lee after his 1981 performance in 'A Soldier's Play.' He was cast in minor roles for movies like 'School Daze' and 'Do The Right Thing,' which was significant enough to grab the attention of Spike Lee.

The 1990s were a golden era in the acting career of Jackson. He earned considerable recognition through performances in 'Goodfellas,' 'True Romance,' 'Jurassic Park,' and 'Amos and Andrew', to name a few. The star also became part of top-rated franchises like George Lucas' 'Star Wars' in the character of the Jedi Master Mace Windu. Among his other famous 1990s, movies are 'A Time to Kill' (1996) and 'The Negotiator' (1998). The star also had a mind-blowing performance in movies like 'Snakes On A Plane,' released in 2006, and 'Kong: Skull Island,' which came out in 2017.

His fame catapulted when he became part of the Marvel franchise with the iconic character of Nick Fury. Every time he played this role, his salary was sure to be above $4 million to $6 million. Marvel has been one of the highest-grossing movie franchises in his entire acting career. 'Avengers: Endgame,' released in 2019, has a lifetime gross of over $803,000,000.

Jackson is particularly famous for working with the talented director Quentin Tarantino. Among his best hits are 'Pulp Fiction' released in 1994, 'Jackie Brown' in 1997, 'Django Unchained' in 2012, and 'The Hateful Eight' in 2015. Pulp Fiction is particularly famous because it also earned Jackson a nomination for the Oscars, as stated by Yahoo! Finance.

Business Ventures

Samuel  has also had a brilliant career as a voice actor. Among his best works as a voice artist is 'Turbo' released in 2013, 'Star Wars: The Clone Wars' released in 2008, and the 'Incredibles' movie part 1 and 2. Among his audiobook recordings is 'God for the New Testament,' an audiobook version of the Bible known as 'The Bible Experience,' according to Inspirationfeed.

The legendary star has also earned millions from endorsements. He has worked with prestigious brands like Adidas, Apple, and Brioni, but his most lucrative endorsement to date has been Capital One. Given that the 71-year-old actor is a pretty well-known face, brands have been hiring him to represent their products and make fabulous sales. His endorsement deals earned him a jaw-dropping $30 million by 2017, making him one of the highest-paid actors in Hollywood.

An incredible net worth of about $250 million has made Samuel Jackson set up an empire comprising lavish estates. Along with his spouse, Jackson owns several luxury mansions that are collectively worth about $30 million. The fact that he can earn from $10 million to $30 million from starring in one movie makes it evident that he is one of the highest-grossing actors in Hollywood cinema.
