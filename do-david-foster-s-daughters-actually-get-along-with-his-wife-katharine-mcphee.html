Do David Foster's Daughters Actually Get Along With His Wife Katharine McPhee?

Even though Yolanda Foster quit RHOBH a long time ago, fans still remember watching theReal Housewives cast member during her marriage to David Foster. Yolanda was always super sweet to David, making him dinner and showering him with love. It was a big shock to find out that they were going to get divorced. And for many fans, it was also surprising to hear that David Foster was engaged to Katharine McPhee, who is several decades younger than him.

Katharine and David are now happily married and the parents to baby boy Rennie. And Katharine is now the stepmother of David's daughters: 51-year-old Allison, 48-year-old Amy, 40-year-old Sara, 39-year-old Erin, and 35-year-old Jordan. But do David's kids actually like his wife? Keep reading to find out if David Foster's daughters actually get along with his wife Katharine McPhee.

Do David Foster's Daughters Like His Wife?

While people focus on the many years between the couple, David Foster and Katharine McPhee's relationship is very loving. And it looks like David Foster's kids are totally fine with his wife Katharine McPhee... but they think that she's really attractive.

Jordan, Sara, and Erin commented on Katharine McPhee's appearance. According to People, Erin explained, "Our biggest issue with [Katharine] is how hot she is. Her body is insane."

Erin also shared that her husband agrees that Katharine is really good-looking: "When she comes over for family Sundays, I'm like, 'Do not get in a bikini.' My husband's always like, 'Kat, get in the pool!' And I'm like, 'Simon...'"

David Foster shares the sentiment: when Katharine shared a photo holding their baby on social media, he wrote in the comments, "HOT MOM!!!!!" with a fire emoji.

Erin explained to People that Katharine is a "facilitator" and if David Foster argues with his children, Katharine encourages him to call them and make up.

Erin Foster talked about Katharine McPhee and had nothing but sweet words to say about her stepmom. She told Us Weekly that Katharine fit in with everyone "effortlessly" and that she is "strong" because marrying someone with grown-up kids isn't ever going to be a simple task. She also noted that David was married four times before Katharine. Erin said, "we really genuinely love Katharine."

Many were surprised by the age difference between the couple since David is 72 years old and Katharine McPhee is 37. There was a bit of an age gap between David and Yolanda Hadid as well, as Yolanda is 57 years old.

According to Us Weekly, David Foster wants to keep his divorce from Yolanda private and doesn't want to tell anyone about any details. He explained in David Foster: Off The Record, “How can I leave a sick woman? The fact of the matter is, that is not the reason why I left. It was for a different reason, which I will never disclose, but it had nothing to do with her being sick."

Sara And Erin Foster Had A Show Called 'Barely Famous'

Sara and Erin Foster seem super close and they also had their own show on VH1 called Barely Famous. The show had two seasons and 12 episodes in total.

According to People, the sisters would love to do more episodes: in 2020, Erin said, “People are always asking for it to come back. I think we realized that we’ve done a bunch of different things and had success since the show, but the truth is, at the root of our partnership together, I think people just want to see Barely Famous again.”

When Erin and Sara Foster talked about Barely Famous in an interview with Refinery 29, they said that it seemed fun to do a fictional TV show that felt like a reality series.

Erin explained, "People in middle America get a taste of the funny things that happen in L.A., and people in L.A. watch stuff they relate to: People setting up paparazzi shoots for themselves, or having a publicist when there’s no work to be done, or having an assistant when they don’t need an assistant. Just the idiots we all know.”

The sisters have some acting credits to their names as well: most notably, Erin appeared on The O.C. and Sara played Jen Clark on the reboot of 90210.

Katharine McPhee is also famous for her musical TV series Smash, which aired for two seasons. She starred in the 2021 Netflix comedy Country Comfort and also played Jenna Hunterson in the Broadway musical Waitress in 2019 and 2020. Most recently, she starred in the movie Tiger Rising, which will come out in 2022, alongside Dennis Quaid and Queen Latifah.
