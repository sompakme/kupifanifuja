Morena Baccarin Makes Slim Post-Baby Body Red Carpet Debut

 Posted on July 3, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  234 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Back at it! Morena Baccarin made her post-baby body red carpet debut at the Fox upfronts in NYC on Monday, May 16, and her supportive partner, Ben McKenzie, was right by her side.

The Deadpool actress, 36, looked slim in a cobalt blue dress by Shoshanna that gathered at her natural waistline. She paired the vibrant frock with a black clutch, embellished black pumps and John Hardy earrings and rings.

The mom of two, who gave birth to baby girl Frances Laiz Setta Schenkkan on March 2, glowed with rosy cheeks and a subtle smoky eye as she smiled for cameras. Baccarin, who shares son Julius, 2, with ex-husband Austin Chick, wore her hair in soft, romantic curls to complete the look.

Her love and Gotham costar, 37, was arm-in-arm with her on the carpet in a tailored blue suit, which he paired with a button-down and dapper lace-up dress shoes

Baccarin opened up about her pregnancy on February 8. “[I’m] so excited! I’m ready,” she told Us Weekly at her Deadpool premiere in NYC.

“I’m a million months pregnant,” she added to Access Hollywood. “With the first one you think of all the things that can go wrong because that’s what the books tell you. And the second you’re just, ‘OK, I’ve done this before. My body will take care of me. You’re in good hands.'”
