Comedian Kountry Wayne Details $200K Monthly Child Support Payments

 Posted on August 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  274 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Kountry Wayne discussed the hefty price tag for his monthly child support payments. The comedian shared the dollar amount as a guest of the Sherri Shepherd Show during an interview with the host. In their conversation, the Praise This actor revealed the total he pays for 10 children, whom he shares with five different women.

“Yeah, I splurge… more than child support? Because child support would be my biggest splurge,” he explains after Shepherd inquires about his spending habits.

“Ok, you splurge on child support? How much is your child support? Can I ask you that?” Shepherd responds.

“Yeah, my child support.. Just my family costs me about 200 a month,” Wayne replies, clarifying the expense as $200,000.

He continued to elaborate, “It don’t have to cost me that much. It’s just child support come with baby mama support. Everybody come behind the children with their hands out.”

In the comedian’s first-ever comedy special, A Woman’s Prayer, which debuted on Netflix last month, Wayne brings light to his unique situation. The platform describes the release as a “rousing stand-up set about life as a dad of 10, how to know if a woman likes you, and why he keeps it real with Jesus.”

According to Deadline, the special was filmed at the Warner Theatre in Washington, D.C., and directed by Jeff Tomsic. Earlier this year, the 35-year-old set out on the HELP IS ON THE WAY Comedy Tour, releasing a book of the same name. The written work, officially titled Help Is on the Way: Stay Up and Live Your Truth and released in April 2023, features a foreward written by Cedric the Entertainer.
