Victoria Pickett is a Canadian soccer player

She plays as a midfielder for Kansas City in the National Women’s Soccer League (NWSL).

Victoria, in her initial days, played college soccer for the Wisconsin Badgers.

Moreover, she is also a former Canada Youth international player, who participated in the 2015 Pan American Games and two FIFA Cups.

In 2018, Pickett made the women’s semifinalist list for the Hermann Trophy, an annual award for college soccer players.

Despite a knee injury in 2019, she appeared in 57 games across her college career, scoring 5 goals and providing 11 assists.
