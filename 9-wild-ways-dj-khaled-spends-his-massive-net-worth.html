9 Wild Ways DJ Khaled Spends His Massive Net Worth

 Posted on August 26, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  898 words  |  Larita Shotwell

DJ Khaled is one of the most prominent producers in the world. Although the record label executive has achieved outstanding success in his career, it wasn't always roses for him. Khaled had come a long way from his early days, when he started with a job at a record store, Odyssey, to becoming a radio host in the late 90s before gaining considerable recognition as DJ/ producer for the Terror Squad.

Khaled translated his success with the group into working on his projects. And in over 16 years, with 13 studio albums to his credit, the musician has become a formidable force in the industry. Khaled's hard work has earned him a fortune, and the larger-than-life entertainer doesn't hold back on spending his massive net worth.

9 DJ Khaled Has A Luxury Car Collection

One thing that points clearly to his impressive $75 million net worth is his collection of luxury cars. Like many high-profile entertainers, Khaled's automotive preferences are in the costliest of cars available in limited quantities. Khaled spoke fluent exclusivity when he splurged over $1.3 million on the 2012 Maybach 62S Landaulet, making him one of the eight luxury vehicle owners.

Other luxurious cars in his collection include the Rolls-Royce Phantom EWB VIII, Cadillac Escalade, Rolls-Royce Phantom VIII, Rolls-Royce Dawn, Maybach 57S, and Rolls-Royce Cullinan SUV.

8 DJ Khaled Owns A Personal Champs Sports Store

On first look, the entertainer owning a Champs store appears like a business investment for shoes, but that is miles far from its intended purpose. The store, which is located next to his Miami studio, will function differently than a traditional retail outfit. Access to the store is restricted to Khaled's inner circle, with everything in the outfit going for free. The store could be considered a showroom with Champs stocks placed on constant rotation.

7 DJ Khaled's Mansion

Nothing screams high-end luxury than owning a 10,681-square-foot house on three-quarters of an acre in the prestigious Mulholland Estate community, Beverly Hills. T

he gated community has played and continues to play host to certain A-listers in the entertainment world. Perhaps that was why Khaled paid $10,000,000 for the mansion in 2016. Five years later, Khaled unloaded his sprawling Beverly Hills mansion for $12.5 million, which implies that he made a profit of over $2 million.

6 DJ Khaled's Expensive Shoe Collection

Shoes speak louder than words, and Khaled understands this to the T. The Grammy-award winner is particularly in love with sneakers - he once spent $25,000 on these shoes. With a sneaker room dedicated to his over 10,000 pairs, multiple collaborations with brands, and an estimated $8 million worth of footwear, it is safe to call the record executive a verified sneaker head.

5 DJ Khaled Is Crazy About Jewelry Pieces

Khaled cannot be found in the number when it comes to subtlety, and his jewelry pieces prove it. While he may not be among celebrities with the most extensive collection of jewels and diamonds, his collection is almost as impressive.

The musician is always donned in the boldest diamond-crested jewelry to accentuate his flamboyance. Among his jewelry collection is the 14k gold ruby 'We The Best' pendant, which probably costs more than your house.

4 DJ Khaled Enjoys Expensive Holidays

The record label executive knows how to put in the work, but when it comes to holidays, he plays just as hard as he works. Like most of his lifestyle choices, Khaled's vacation options embody luxury and exclusivity.

In 2019, the musician enjoyed a family vacation at Rio Chico Villa, Jamaica - an exclusive 14-acre private property costing a whopping $6,430 per night.

3 DJ Khaled Made A Custom Jacket Out Of Scarves

The year is 2016, and everyone, including Goyard, is wondering why Khaled is wearing a 'fake' Goyard bomber jacket. It turned out that the musician had a custom jacket made out of silk scarves by the luxury brand.

The jacket was made with around 3-5 yards of the scarf, with each one retailing at $1,795. Following the uproar, GQ's analysis of everything to know about DJ Khaled's costly "fake" Goyard jacket pegged the amount of making the custom bomber jacket at approximately $30,000.

2 DJ Khaled Splurges On Presents

Whether it's for his friends or family, one thing is sure; the celebrity disc jockey goes all out when it comes to gifting. To celebrate his first son Asahd's birthday, the joyous father gifted his son a Frank Muller watch worth US$ 100,000.

Khaled's generosity extends to friends, team, and charity. Some of his outrageous generosity includes gifting Stormi Webster her first Chanel bag, gifting his team $10,000 Rolex watches, and giving $100,000 in scholarships to high school students in Miami.

1 DJ Khaled Owns A Private Jet

For years, Khaled nurtured a deep-rooted fear of flying, so much so that he avoided traveling by air for ten years. But all that changed when he became a father; he conquered his fear of flying and acquired a Gulfstream G650 private jet to suit his luxurious lifestyle.

In classic Khaled fashion, the musician is looking to make a fleet; in October, he was spotted shopping for a VIP Boeing 767.
