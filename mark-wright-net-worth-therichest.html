Mark Wright born in 1987 in Buckhurst Hill, Essex, is a renowned English reality TV celebrity and a radio presenter. His net worth stands at $4 million.

Josh Wright the football player is his younger brother. His personal love life was not that smooth as his engagement was broken in 2011 with actress Lauren Goodger on allegations that Lauren was unfaithful in the relationship. However, things took a turn, when in the Essex show it was revealed that Mark was the actual culprit, as he cheated Lauren because he had a relationship with Lucy Mecklenburgh who interestingly was his previous girlfriend and a co-star as well. Since January 2013, Mark has been in a relationship with the Coronation Street actress Michelle Keegan.

He came into recognition after being an important cast member in The Only Way Is Essex (TOWIE). However, he announced the intention of leaving the show when the third series ended on October 23rd, 2011. Wright made a cameo appearance in the sixth series and also did the Christmas special 2012 series. His next participation was in the eleventh series of I'm a Celebrity...Get Me out of Here. He came second in the show behind Dougie Poynter.

On 2011, Wright presented jointly the Take Me Out spin-off series Take Me Out: The Gossip. On December 21st, 2011, it was confirmed that Mark would become a health expert for ITV Breakfast program Daybreak. In 2012, Wright featured in a new ITV2 show titled Mark Wright's Hollywood Nights.

Before his appearance in TOWIE, he was a semi-professional footballer.
