Demi Moore is a renowned actress, producer, and former model known for her long and illustrious career in Hollywood. She is often regarded as one of the most famous and desired actresses of all time. Throughout her career, she has made headlines not just for her work but also for her personal life. Moore has been married thrice and has been in several high-profile relationships.

Demi Moore's dating history starts when she was 18 and includes renowned names like Harry Morton, Bruce Willis, Ashton Kutcher, etc.

Freddy Moore - $1 Million

Freddy Moore was a musician primarily active during the 80s. Demi Moore married Freddy in 1980 at the age of just 18, as reported by Hollywood Life. The couple was married for five years, from 1980 to 1985. And Demi, whose original surname was Guynes, got the ‘Moore’ surname from Freddy which she uses to date. Freddy Moore, throughout his career, wrote the lyrics for almost 1,000 original songs, formed multiple bands, and even starred in a few film roles. He passed away in 2022, leaving behind a $1 Million fortune.

Dweezil Zappa - $4 Million

Following her split from Emilio Estevez, Demi Moore began a brief relationship with rock star Dweezil Zappa in 1987. The couple's romantic fling quickly fizzled out, and they parted ways without any significant drama. Although they didn't date long, the two were frequently spotted together in public, with paparazzi catching them on several outings. Zappa is the son of acclaimed musician Frank Zappa and has been affiliated with over five record labels garnering him an adequate $4 Million net worth.

Martin Henderson - $6 million

Demi Moore was in a relationship with New Zealand actor Martin Henderson from 2012 to 2013. Henderson is best known for his roles in the television shows Grey's Anatomy and Shortland Street. He has also appeared in films such as The Ring and Torque. With his frequent appearances in major movies and TV shows, Martin Henderson’s current net worth stands at $5 million as per Celebrity Net Worth.

Daniel Humm - $15 Million

Demi Moore returned to the dating scene in early 2022 and started a romance with renowned Swiss chef Daniel Humm, aged 46. The couple was in the front row at the Chloe Womenswear show during Paris Fashion Week. They spent romantic moments at Humm's restaurant, Eleven Madison Park, where he often works long hours. The duo seemed to be getting closer, but their love story was short-lived. In November 2022, Us Weekly confirmed that Demi and Daniel had gone their separate ways. With his excellent restaurant business, Daniel Humm has garnered a $15 Million fortune.

Emilio Estevez - $18 Million

Emilio Estevez, the actor, and director, was Demi Moore's first high-profile boyfriend in the 1980s. The two met on the set of St. Elmo's Fire in 1985 and were together for two years. Estevez is known for his roles in iconic movies such as The Breakfast Club, Young Guns, and The Mighty Ducks. He has an estimated net worth of an impressive $18 million.

Harry Morton - $100 Million

Harry Morton, the founder of the famous Pink Taco restaurants, was Demi Moore's boyfriend in 2013. Morton and Moore were together briefly until May 2013 before his untimely death in 2019. Morton was the son of Peter Morton, the co-founder of the Hard Rock Cafe. He had an estimated net worth of $100 million.

Ashton Kutcher - $200 Million

One of the better-known faces on this list, Ashton Kutcher, is an actor, entrepreneur, and Demi Moore's third husband. The couple married in 2005, despite their 16-year age difference, and were together for eight years before divorcing in 2013. Kutcher is known for his roles in That 70s Show, Two and a Half Men, and The Ranch. He has an estimated net worth of an astounding $200 million, as per Parade owing to his lucrative movie career and multiple brand endorsements.

Bruce Willis - $250 million

Demi Moore started dating Bruce Willis, one of the highest-paid actors of this generation, in August 1987, after meeting him at a screening of the film Stakeout. The couple married on November 21, 1987, and had three daughters. The couple became one of the most powerful in Hollywood, frequently working together in films. They became a major tabloid sensation, with their every move followed and reported on in the media. Unfortunately, the power couple got divorced in 2000. Over his long career, Bruce Willis has appeared in several era-defining movies like Die Hard, The Sixth Sense, Armageddon, etc., accumulating a stunning $250 Million net worth.

Demi Moore has had several high-profile relationships throughout her career. While some of her boyfriends have been more successful than others, all of them have made their mark in their respective industries. Her relationship with Bruce Willis was one of the most high-profile and enduring in Hollywood, and his stunning $250 Million net worth reflects his success in the entertainment industry.
