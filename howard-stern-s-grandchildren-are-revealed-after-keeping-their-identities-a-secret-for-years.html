Howard Stern's Grandchildren Are Revealed After Keeping Their Identities A Secret For Years

 Posted on July 30, 2024  |  7 minutes  |  1302 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Highlights

  • Howard Stern revealed he has a granddaughter named Naomi, and a baby grandson, both from his daughters.
  • Stern is finally candid about his relationships with his grandchildren, but struggles to relate to babies.
  • He shared details of his grandson's briss ceremony, showing newfound openness about his family life.

Howard Stern made an absolute fortune by exposing the gritty details of his life for over 40 years. However, he's particularly quiet about the personal lives of his three daughters, Emily, Deborah, and Ashley; with whom he had with his first wife, Alison Berns.

Stern decided not to have any more children when he married Beth, his second wife who is 18 years his junior.

As Stern's daughters have aged, he has been more transparent about their lives, especially as far as his youngest daughter, Ashley, is concerned. But his two grandchildren are another matter entirely. In this article, we will reveal what little we know about Howard Stern's relationship with his granddaughter, Naomi, and then delve into his newborn grandson.

Howard Stern's Wife Beth Accidentally Revealed He Had A Grandaughter

Howard Stern's granddaughter is named Naomi and she is the child of Deborah Jennifer Stern

Prior to June 2023, Stern Show fans had no idea that the radio legend had a grandchild. It was Howard Stern's wife, Beth, who accidentally spilled the beans. During segments before and after the wedding of Stern's youngest daughter, Ashley, Beth claimed that her step-grandchild was the flower girl.

The first time she mentioned Naomi, who was born to Stern's middle daughter, Deborah, the radio legend was clearly unhappy. So much so that he originally had the replay of the broadcast edited so that Naomi's name wasn't included. But over time, he himself has spoken about his relationship with Naomi, who lives with his daughter's family across the country.

Stern has been less cagey about the June 2024 birth of his grandson, who is the product of Ashley and her husband, Adam Weinstein.

"A couple of days ago, I became a grandfather for the second time. I have a granddaughter and a grandson... My couple runneth over."

On his June 17th, 2024 show, Howard Stern claimed that he never imagined that he'd have grandchildren. When he was younger, he envisioned having children, but nothing ever beyond that. Yet, he's been able to build a strong relationship with his granddaughter, Naomi.

  • Howard Stern originally didn't know what he wanted his grandchildren to call him. While he claimed his wife, Beth, wasn't thrilled with being called 'grandma', Stern decided to be called 'grandpa'.

Howard Stern doesn't get to see Naomi as much as he'd like but has shared that they get along really well. In fact, Stern is often taken aback by their close connection. While Naomi is still very young, Stern says he entertains her by singing and doing puppet shows with her -- something he used to do himself as a child.

Naomi also loves spending time with all of the adopted cats and rabbits that populate all three of Howard Stern's properties.

Beth Stern runs Beth's Furry Friends out of the North Shore Animal League. At any given time, Howard Stern and his wife could have upwards of 30 cats in their home. Some are theirs, while others are looking for homes.

Since Howard Stern has only now been open about Naomi, very little else is known about their relationship. However, this will likely change in the years to come.

Howard Stern Has A Baby Grandson Who He Struggles To Relate To

Howard Stern's grandson was born to his youngest daughter, Ashley Jade in June 2024

Despite it taking a couple of years for Howard Stern to reveal that he had a granddaughter, the radio legend was far quicker to inform his audience of his grandson's arrival. He did so in June 2024 during a discussion about his weekend activities. Without a doubt, Stern seemed elated to have a baby grandson despite the fact he, admittedly, doesn't know how to deal with babies.

Stern's candidness was likely because his grandson was born to Ashley Jade. Ashley has been far more comfortable with her personal life being talked about on The Howard Stern Show in contrast with her two sisters. After all, she let her father and stepmother publicly discuss the events of her June 2023 wedding to Adam Weinstein in detail. Deborah, on the other hand, likes her privacy and her former shock jock father respects that.

Ashley Jade is Howard Stern's youngest daughter. She married Adam Weinstein in June 2023. They had their first child in June 2024.

When discussing his newborn grandson with his co-host, Robin Quivers, Stern claimed he felt pride in his youngest daughter, Ashley, after the birth of her son:

"My baby had a baby."

Howard Stern claimed that he spent two days in and around the hospital with his daughter, her husband and his latest grandchild.

"I sat and held my grandson for hours... just hours on end," Stern said to Quivers on his June 17th broadcast. "Then I went back the next day because I couldn't get enough. It was so much fun. I really enjoyed that. He was just great."

"This [grandchild] will be closer, so you can see him more often," Quivers said to her colleague, referring to the fact that Stern's middle daughter lives in California with his other grandchild, Naomi.

Following Stern's hospital visit, he participated in a North Shore Animal League gala. Because of this, he refused to return to visit his grandson despite his daughter requesting his presence. This is because Stern's still very much afraid of COVID-19 due to his germaphobia.

On his radio show, Stern claimed that he didn't want to give his newborn grandson anything if he picked it up from being around so many people. While he would argue that he was being considerate, others would call him "crazy". Especially since his daughter Ashley forced him to get over his crippling disease in time for her wedding in June 2023.

But Stern's refusal to return to visit his grandson right away may also have to do with his difficulty relating to babies. While speaking to Robin Quivers, Stern joked that his days of taking care of babies are over. Stern can spend hours with his toddler granddaughter but finds communicating with babies far more challenging. He even joked that he wanted them to watch John Wick and Godzilla Minus One together.

"I can't take care of little babies. I'm good for two hours. I sat there. I held him and rocked him."

A week after the birth of his grandson, Stern revealed the details of the briss. In the Jewish ethno-religion, a briss is the circumcision ceremony that is typically for the family and close friends. Stern stated that their briss was a small family affair that included his ex-wife, Alison Berns. The radio legend also worried that his grandson's 'calm disposition' would be disturbed by the ceremony. Fortunately, the urologist who did it was fast and supportive.

Given how transparent Howard Stern has suddenly become about his grandchildren, it appears his fans will learn far more about them in the coming years.
