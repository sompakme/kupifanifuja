Who are Michael Porter Jr.’s parents?

Former NBA player Brandon Roy was the coach of Nathan Hale’s basketball team; his father, Michael Porter Sr., was appointed as an assistant coach by the University of Washington. His mother, Lisa Porter, holds an MBA and a stellar athletic resume.

At six foot four, Lisa Porter remembers always excelling at the sport. For her high school team, Jefferson High, in Cedar Rapids, Iowa, she averaged 58.7 points per game as a senior in 1983; she is confident that her son won’t be able to achieve that mark in the intensely competitive NBA.

Porter played basketball at the University of Iowa after graduating high school, where she amassed 1,335 points in three seasons.
