The upcoming movie Cloudy with a Chance of Christmas includes a group of talented casts of Valery M. Ortiz, Brandon Quinn, Sarah Jane Morris, and many more. 

The movie is written and directed by Jake Helgren, the 41-year-old American film producer, screenwriter, and director. He has directed and written movies like Christmas on the Menu, Dashing in December, The Party Planner, and many more. He is also the writer of Fatal Fandom, Love Exclusively, and 10 Year Reunion, among others. 

He has also worked on over fifty movies, series, and videos as a producer, like Tow, Writing Around the Christmas Tree, Fight for Justice: Billy’s Story, A Walk Down Wedding Lane, A Walk Down Wedding Lane., and others. 

The movie’s story starts with Bridget Torres, played by Valery Ortiz, being asked to host the annual snow forecast of her network in the cold and wintry town of Leavenworth. She is asked to have the forecast live with the morning show host Drake Kincaid played by Brandon Quinn. He is her long-time rival after some time, tension arises, and romance sparks between them. 

Cloudy With A Chance Of Christmas Cast

The main cast of the upcoming movie, Cloudy With A Chance Of Christmas includes Valery M. Ortiz, Brandon Quinn, Sarah Jane Morris, and Nicole Bilderback, among others.

1. Valery Milagros Ortiz as Bridget Torress

Who is Bridget Toress? Bridget is a national news weather forecaster who goes to Leavenworth, Washington for the Christmas traditions that happen each year. She is asked to host the special snow cast for the annual Christmas Eve festival with her Lisa. The talk show host from her network refused to work with her a year ago. They seem to be romantically involved after some time.

Where have I seen Valery Ortiz before? The 38-year-old Puerto Rican actress has appeared in various TV series like Gabby Duran & the Unsittables, Hit the Floor, South of Nowhere, and Dairy of a Single Mom. She has also worked on films such as Thriller, Date Movie, Dumbbells, and various other movies.  

2. Brandon Quinn as Drake Kincaid

Who is Drake Kincaid? Drake is the talk show host of the Christmas Eve festival. He refuses to work with Bridget as a co-host on the morning show a year before. He finds himself falling for the main character of the movie. However, tension arises after she finds out that he refused to work with her. Their romantic life is put to a test when she finds out about last year’s incident.

Where have I seen Brandon Quinn before? The American TV and film actor, Brandon Quinn has worked in the movies and series such as The Nightmare Room, What I Like About You, CSI: Crime Scene Investigation, Charmed, Sweet Magnolias, Big Wolf of Campus, Malachance, and Chicken Soup for the Soul. He started his career with the Charles Murphy film Express: Aisle to Glory. 

3. Sarah Jane Morris as Tatum Tilson

Who is Tatum Tilson? Tatum Tilson is the co-host of the network’s show. She works with Drake as well as Bridget and all of them are together in the snow cast for the Christmas Eve festival. 

Where have I seen Sarah Jane Morris before? Sarah is mostly known for her role in the ABC television drama Brothers & Sisters as Julia Walker which aired from 2006 to 2009. She has also worked on movies and series such as Coyote Ugly, 6 Month Rule, Law & Order: Criminal Intent, The Odds, and many more. 

4. Nicole Bilderback as Lisa Liu Lawson

Who is Lisa Liu Lawson? Lisa is Bridget Torres’s boss who goes to Leavenworth, Washington, California, to help manage the special snow cast at the annual Christmas Eve Festival that happens every holiday season. 

Where have I seen Nicole Bilderback before? The 47-year-old actress is known for her recurring role in the TV programs Dawson’s creek and Dark Angels. She started her career in the year 1995 and is famous for her portrayal in movies like Bring It On, The Box, Cruel World, and Bad Girls from Valley High.

Cloudy With A Chance of Christmas Filmed In Leavenworth WA

The Hallmark movie Cloudy with a Chance of Christmas was filmed in Leavenworth, Washington, the United States. 

The movie was reportedly shot in Leavenworth around last year. The city is Chelan County, Washington.

The entire town center is built on the German Bavarian village, a part of a civic initiative that started in the 1960s. Most of the city of buildings are modeled after Bavarian settlements. 

The city opened Leavenworth Nutcracker Museum in 1995 which is very famous in the city. The museum contains more than six thousand tools that were invented for cracking nuts that dated back to Roman times. The city has beautiful snowy winter which is what the movie is revolved around. The movie is based on the snow cast of the magical wintry town of Leavenworth. 

The city is filled with attractions like Leavenworth Reindeer Farm, Icicle Gorge, Eagle Creek Ranch, Waterfront Park, Front Street Park, and many more. 

Valery Ortiz’s Instagram posted a photo in the New Year and the location was filled with snow in Leavenworth, Washington. The movie is filled with beautiful scenes and snowy houses.
