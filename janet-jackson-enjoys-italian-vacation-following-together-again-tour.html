Janet Jackson Enjoys Italian Vacation Following Together Again Tour

 Posted on August 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  291 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Janet Jackson has been living her best life while on her summer vacation with close friends Tasha Smith and Chris Rios Riggio.

On Wednesday (Aug. 30), the Velvet Rope singer shared highlights from the tail-end of her European adventure in Puglia, Italy.

“We rounded out our summer vacation at the breathtaking @Tenuta_Negroamaro in Puglia, Italy,” she wrote on Instagram, referring to the boutique hotel. “The hotel grounds are so peaceful & relaxing and it’s such a blessing to be able to spend quality time with my baby.”

Jackson shared a series of photos of the breathtaking scenery as fans got a rare vantage point into her trip. The week prior, she and her girls visited Portugal and the “All For You” singer raved about the village of Melides and its beautiful architecture, people, and shops, including one named Vida Dura that “sells beautiful hand painted home decor and gorgeous flowers.”

She stayed at Hotel Vermelho, which is owned by Christian Louboutin.

The mother of one was also introduced to the Black-owned brand, Black Girl Sunscreen by Smith during their “Italian girls trip.”

“While it was made with people of color in mind, it can be used on every skin tone and feels so good. So worth supporting [heart emoji] Wishing the very best to all the entrepreneurs out there,” wrote Jackson in her unofficial endorsement.

Jackson’s trip followed the conclusion of her Together Again Tour. Accompanied by Ludacris and other surprise guests like Busta Rhymes and Jermaine Dupri, the 33-city tour was described by VIBE as a “celebration for die-hard fans” as the singer guided attendees through her storied catalog with live renditions of “What Have You Done For Me Lately,” “Nasty,” “Any Time, Any Place,” “I Get Lonely,” “Scream,” “Rhythm Nation,” and more.
