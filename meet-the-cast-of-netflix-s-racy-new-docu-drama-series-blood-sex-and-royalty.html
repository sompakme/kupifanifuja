Meet The Cast Of Netflix's Racy New Docu-Drama Series, Blood Sex And Royalty

 Posted on August 18, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  876 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The Netflix original series Blood, Sex & Royalty offers viewers a modern take on the British monarchy, offering a glimpse into the lives of history's deadliest, sexiest, and most iconic monarchs.In a similar manner to The Crown, which recently released its fifth season, Blood, Sex & Royalty follows Anne Boleyn, King Henry VIII's second of six wives. As the woman who would partly lead to the split between the King and the Church, became a symbol of feminist ideals, and inspire various saucy rumors, Anne Boleyn has inspired a number of films, Broadway productions and TV shows including a mini-series with Jodie Turner-Smith in the titular role that was met with racist backlash. There is even a chilling tale of Anne Boleyn's 'headless' ghost that haunts her place of birth.Blood, Sex & Royalty combines historians' knowledge with steamy scripted drama, offering a glimpse into the lives of the Tudor monarchs and the noble house of Boleyn. The first season of Blood, Sex & Royalty only has three episodes, and there’s no telling if more will be added or if the series will get a second season. Though, if it is renewed by Netflix it is likely to be released in the fall of 2023.Blood, Sex & Royalty features two British soap opera stars as its leads; you might recognize them, as well as the other cast members. Let's see who is part of the cast of Blood, Sex & Royalty and where you’ve seen the actors before.

9 Who Plays Henry Tudor In Blood, Sex & Royalty?

Max Parker is fairly new to the industry, with his earliest project being My B.F.F in 2016. The British actor has booked a handful of roles since then including Peacock’s for the adaptation of Vampire Academy, which recently wrapped up its first season. Parker, who plays Mikhail Tanner in the series based on the novels by Richelle Mead, portrays Henry Tudor in Blood, Sex & Royalty.

8 This Actress Portrays Anne Boleyn In Blood, Sex & Royalty

Amy James-Kelly plays Anne Boleyn the famous second wife of King Henry VIII in the series, but she’s had quite a career so far since her 2013 debut on the TV series Coronation Street.

She is known for her portrayals of Jenny in Safe, Sarah in the Military Wives movie, and most recently as Suzannah Washington/Suzannah Sowden in Gentleman Jack.

7 Who Plays Thomas Boleyn In Blood, Sex & Royalty?

Thomas Boleyn was an English Nobleman, and also the father of Queen Anne Boleyn. Adam Astill portrays the Earl in Blood, Sex & Royalty, but he’s previously starred in Mistresses from 2008-2010 along with other TV series including Holby City (2011-2012), and EastEnders(2005-2017).

6 Who Plays Lady Mary In Blood, Sex & Royalty?

Lois Brabin-Platt is known for her roles in Eb and Flo (2019), The Bezonians (2021) and Righteous Villains (2020). The British actress plays Mary Boleyn or Lady Mary, the sister of Anne Boleyn in the series. Mary was also one of Henry Tudor’s mistresses for an unknown period of time.

5 This Scottish Actor Plays George Boleyn In Blood, Sex & Royalty

Jhon Lumsden is a Scottish actor whose musical theater credits include Heathers, and Les Misérables. The actor who plays George Boleyn in the Netflix series, is also known for his 2019 appearance as Harvey Hodges on Doctors (which is currently in its 23rd season), as well as appearances on the TV short Pancake, and the TV series Karen Pirie.

4 Who Plays Jane Boleyn In Blood, Sex & Royalty?

The German-British actress only recently started acting professionally, after receiving her Masters in Screen Acting from Drama Centre in London. This would mark Sophie Boettge's biggest role so far after starring in the short film The Escape Room in 2020. In Blood, Sex & Royalty, Sophie plays Jane Boleyn, who was married to George Boleyn, the brother of Anne Boleyn.

3 The Actress Who Portrays Lady Worcester Is Also New To The Industry

Nikhita Lesler who portrays Lady Worcester in Blood, Sex, & Royalty is also new to the industry. Nikhita made her TV debut portraying Aliya Azad in BBC's Casualty, and has since filmed for BBC’s The Sixth Commandment.

2 This Actor Who Plays Thomas Wyatt Also Appeared In Another Popular Series

He stars as politician Thomas Wyatt in Blood, Sex & Royalty but just a few weeks ago Aron von Adrian appeared as one of the Velaryon Knights in HBO’s House of the Dragon. The actor is also known for playing a promoter in the channel 4 comedy We Are Lady Parts and many other movies and TV series.

Other actors in the cast of Blood, Sex & Royalty includes actor/producer Steffan Boje, and Callum Coates who’s appeared in Starz’ Outlander and Netflix’s The Witcher starring Henry Cavill, who recently announced hes leaving the show.
