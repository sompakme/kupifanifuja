From golf course design and apparel to hospitality and philanthropy, Woods' entrepreneurial ventures mirror his commitment to excellence and innovation.

Tiger Woods' earnings throughout his career have been multifaceted, with his success as a professional golfer forming the cornerstone. His exceptional golfing skills and the accompanying endorsements have significantly contributed to his wealth. Wealth that has reached $1.1 billion.

Beyond golf, Woods' business ventures span various sectors, including golf course design, hospitality, apparel, and philanthropy. While he has had major controversy with his marriage, Tiger Woods' legacy resonates not only with his achievements on the course but also with his entrepreneurial spirit and impact on the business world.

10 Tiger Woods' Earnings From Golf: A Phenomenal Career

Earnings: $157 Million From Golf Wins

  • Woods earned $121 million from tournaments alone

Tiger Woods' primary source of income stems from his remarkable career in professional golf. Bursting onto the scene in the late 1990s, Woods quickly became a sensation with his unprecedented skill and dominance. His numerous victories on the PGA Tour, including 15 major championships, have earned him significant prize money. Major wins, such as his historic triumphs at the Masters and the U.S. Open, have come with substantial monetary rewards, further contributing to his financial success.

These winnings, throughout Woods' career, have earned him over $157 million. From tournaments alone, Woods has earned $121 million. However, since 2020, Woods has not earned more than $100,000 from tournament participation.

Woods' prowess on the golf course also led to lucrative endorsement deals with major brands. Companies like Nike, Rolex, and Bridgestone have eagerly aligned themselves with Woods, capitalizing on his global popularity and unrivaled golfing reputation. These endorsement deals have been a substantial source of income for Woods, turning him into one of the highest-earning athletes of all time.

8 Tiger Woods' Restaurant: The Woods Jupiter

Earnings From The Woods Jupiter: Unknown

  • Woods spent $8 million to open The Jupiter Woods

Tiger Woods' business ventures extend beyond the golf course, including forays into the hospitality industry. He has been associated with restaurant ventures that cater to both his love for golf and his passion for culinary experiences.

The Woods Jupiter, a restaurant located in Florida, offers patrons a unique blend of gourmet dining and golf-themed ambiance.
