Who Is Lauren Cohan?

Lauren Cohan is an actress famous for starring in the AMC horror series The Walking Dead.

Born on January 7, 1982, in New Jersey, Cohan first appeared onscreen in the 2005 film, Casanova, and later went on to appear in Van Wilder: The Rise of Taj, Float, Death Race 2, and The Boy.

Cohan had her college education at the University of Winchester, where she studied drama and English literature.

In the AMC series The Walking Dead, Cohan played the role of CIA operative Francesca “Frankie”, and is set to appear in its 10th season.

Cohan is an award-winning actress of the Satellite Awards, Saturn Awards, People’s Choice Awards and Eyegore Awards.

Cohan lives and works in London and the United States.
