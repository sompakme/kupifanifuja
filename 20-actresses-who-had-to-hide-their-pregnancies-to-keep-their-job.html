In Hollywood, there is a trend when actresses hide their pregnancy to continue a project they have been assigned. It is not necessarily a great way to work, but yes for some it is necessary as otherwise they would be dropped from the project. When it comes to a series, there is a great challenge as the actress has worked a long way to gain recognition in the industry while fitting that particular role. When the actress is in the initial months of pregnancy, they never reveal it as they feel they could be dropped from the project. Usually, the actresses never make an official announcement of their pregnancy. The directors and producers have to alter some scenes to hide their baby bump. The creative team places the camera at such an angle that the baby bump is barely visible.

Well, in some cases like a long series, the producers either shorten the role of the actress or rush through filming so that they have a smaller belly to work with. Replacements are sometimes used, a cameo of a new celeb to fill the role but that does not always work with fans. It is no doubt a great challenge for any actress to act normally on the sets and tactically hide their bumps. We have studied certain cases where the actress didn’t reveal her pregnancy but concealed it in a smart way, thereby not letting the public know that she is pregnant.

It’s interesting to know the stories how their pregnancy was actually hidden from the public. Let’s go through such interesting tales and know about the experience of the actors.

Alyson is definitely one heck of an actress. She has the guts to pose in a humorous way and take up challenging things in life. (Source: Businessinsider.com)
