Wealthy celebrities are known to spend a massive income on extravagant things. Some, like Tracee Ellis Ross who has a net worth of $16 million, spend their wealth meekly while others, like Arnold Schwarzenegger, spend theirs outlandishly.

It should come as no surprise that someone of Arnold Schwarzenegger's stature would choose to live life on the more flashy side compared to other celebrities in his industry. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Arnold Schwarzenegger's net worth sits at $450 million. So let's take a look at what The Terminator star spends his colossal net worth on.

6 Arnold Schwarzenegger Splurges On Expensive Watches

Arnold Schwarzenegger owns a custom-made watch worth up to $21,739

Recently, Arnold Schwarzenegger was stopped and detained at customs in Germany for a unique reason: his expensive watch went over the price limit for undeclared items.

AP reported that items and goods valued at $467 (430 euros) that will remain in the European Union are required to be declared along with duty paid on them (where appropriate), according to The Hollywood Reporter.

According to The Hollywood Reporter, Arnold Schwarzenegger arrived in the country and was stopped for a routine check, which soon turned into a three-hour detainment. Although Schwarzenegger's Climate Initiative felt that the detainment was an "incompetent shakedown," the actor reportedly cooperated at every step and had agreed to prepay any potential fees on the watch even though it was a personal watch.

Soon after this encounter made headlines and garnered a lot of controversy, Arnold Schwarzenegger put his watch up for grabs at an auction. According to Yahoo Entertainment, the auction supported the movie mogul's initiative geared toward climate resolution. The watch sold for $293,000 (270,000 euros).

Arnold Schwarzenegger's Climate Initiative (SCI) About

"Builds awareness of the climate crisis and the urgent need for action every day. The focus lies on presenting workable solutions, supporting best in class climate action projects and serving as a 'matchmaker for climate leaders.'"

Arnold Schwarzenegger once owned a mansion worth up to $11 million
