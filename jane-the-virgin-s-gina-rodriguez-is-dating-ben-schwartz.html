Jane the Virgin's Gina Rodriguez Is Dating Ben Schwartz!

 Posted on September 15, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  216 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Off the market! Gina Rodriguez is dating House of Lies actor Ben Schwartz, Us Weekly can exclusively confirm.

“She’s so happy,” a source tells Us. “She thinks he is so great and all her friends love him.”

Schwartz, 34, is also known for playing Jean-Ralphio on Parks and Recreation from 2010 to 2015, and appearing in 2014’s This Is Where I Leave You.

As previously reported, the Jane the Virgin actress, 31, more recently dated Revenge star Henri Esteve. They met at a wrap party, and split in September 2015 after more than a year of dating.

Earlier this month, Rodriguez kept tight-lipped about her budding romance when she dropped by The Ellen DeGeneres Show. She did, however, reveal that she really, really wants a baby. The Golden Globe winner plays a mom on her CW comedy.

“I feel like they bring the baby up to me [on set] and my ovaries are like, ‘Ahh! Come here, baby!'” she told DeGeneres on Thursday, January 21. “It’s, like, perfect. As soon as one cries they take it away from me and the other one comes in. That’s what happens in real life, right?”

She quipped: “I could adopt, right? Yeah, I just might do that. I definitely want to adopt.”
